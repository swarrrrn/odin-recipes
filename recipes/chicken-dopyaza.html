Chicken Dopyaza

Description

Chicken Do Pyaza is a classic North Indian curry in which onions are used in two stages of cooking, giving the dish its name "Do Pyaza," meaning "double onions."

The curry has a wonderful balance of sweetness from the onions and warmth from the spices, making it a comforting and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken
  • 4 onions (2 chopped, 2 cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, then cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook for 8–10 minutes.
  5. Add the cubed onions and mix well.
  6. Cover and cook until the chicken is tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
