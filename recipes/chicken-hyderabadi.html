Chicken Hyderabadi

Description

Chicken Hyderabadi is a spicy and flavorful curry originating from Hyderabad, famous for its bold spices and rich culinary heritage. The dish combines marinated chicken with herbs, yogurt, and traditional Indian spices

Its deep flavors and aromatic gravy make it a favorite accompaniment for biryani, naan, and steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add green chilies and marinated chicken.
  4. Cook on medium heat until the chicken starts releasing moisture.
  5. Add coriander and cumin powder.
  6. Cover and cook until the chicken becomes tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with rice, naan, or biryani.
